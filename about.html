About Me


Hi, I’m Vanshika Rana, a 3rd year B.Tech Computer Science student at Himachal Pradesh Technical University with a curious mind and a passion for exploring knowledge beyond boundaries.

I believe in learning deeply, thinking differently, and building solutions that not only solve problems but also add meaning to people’s lives. My journey so far has been guided by curiosity — whether it’s understanding how people think and grow, exploring the power of data, or experimenting with new technologies.

At this stage, I’m embracing challenges that require creativity, logic, and purpose. I love being around people who inspire me to look at the world differently, and I enjoy working on projects that make me question, reflect, and improve.


Some of my areas of interest include:

  • Data Science & Machine Learning– exploring predictive models and real-world applications.
  • Research & Innovation– diving deep into concepts to uncover meaningful insights.
  • Human-Computer Connection– understanding how technology influences behavior and decision-making.

I’m still at the beginning of my journey, but with every project, I’m growing — not just as a student, but as a thinker and creator. My goal is to blend technology with purpose, and contribute to something bigger than myself.
